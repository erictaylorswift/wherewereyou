Where were you?

Short vignettes of my life as a child.

This site is a collection of short thoughts to help me process my childhood trauma as I was living with parental figures suffering from untreated mental health issues. I do not mean for my content to come off as a pity party, but rather to shed light on the serious affects of mental illness. We need to start recognizing these illnesses more and providing the support people and families need to navigate. Otherwise, I fear, history will continue to repeat itself and the sins of the father (and mother) will be placed on the children.
