Where were you?

Where were you for my sister?

Friday, 10 May, 2019

You let my sister live on the streets at sixteen! Where the hell were you for her?

She was just a child, and you gave up because she wasn't easy to deal with. She was in pain and was confused on how to cope.

Instead of showing patience, or compassion, you gave up and kicked her to the curb to fend for herself; how did you think a child could have the skills and tools to survive?
